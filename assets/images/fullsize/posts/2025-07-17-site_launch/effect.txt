GIMP

1. Capture
    Firefox
        :screenshot --dpr 3 --fullpage

2. Add to GIMP

3. Image > Rotate > Arbitary Rotation: -3

4. Create background layer using color

5. Merge Layer + Background

6. LED screen effect
    a.) Duplicate main layer
    b.) Filters > Distorts > Newsprint
        Color Mode: RGB
        Spot function: Diamond
        Cell Size: 10 (see high resolution)
        Angle: 0 for stripes or 45 degrees for dot patterns

    c.) Change layer mode to Overlay or Soft light (and/or Gaussian blur ~2px)
    d.) Opacity ~60%

7. Flatten layers

8. Focus Blur
    Filters > Blur > Focus Blur
        Lens Blur, not Gaussian
        Pick center point and adjust values.

9. Noise
    Filters → Noise → RGB Noise
        Keep values low

10. Crop to 3840x2160
